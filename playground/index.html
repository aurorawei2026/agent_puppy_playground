🐺🐶🐕‍🦺 Small-puppy Playground

Three puppies, three specialties, one buried bone.

🐺

Scout

Husky

Explorer & communication hub

  • Vision: 7 cells (widest of the three)
  • Smell: none
  • Can dig: no

Maps landmarks, tracks where everyone is, and — crucially — relays Sniffer's bone tip to Digger when they're too far apart to talk.

🐶

Sniffer

Beagle

Bone detector

  • Vision: normal
  • Smell: 4 cells (finds the bone)
  • Can dig: no

Wanders until she catches a scent, then homes in. Shouts scent strength and coordinates so the others can come help.

🐕‍🦺

Digger

Terrier

Excavator

  • Vision: normal
  • Smell: none
  • Can dig: yes — only him

Listens for coordinates in chat. The only puppy who can actually dig up the bone when he gets there.

To run your own: python -m sim.run --no-llm --difficulty medium --seed 13 --ticks 60
